Online Bank

Description:

Final Project for the Software Engineering course at SJSU, in which five students needed to implement and deliver a web application that supported basic banking transaction requests. We designed the project based on a client-server architecture. Two students focused on the back-end technologies(PHP), two other students focused on the front-end(React), and one final member would be an intermediary between the two ends. I worked as the intermediary as well as designed the relation database model using MySQL. We followed agile methodologies to maintain a consistent pace throughout the development and were able to successfully finish the project by the end of the semester. A team member also made a demo, as shown below.

Achievements:

  • Collaborated with a team of five to develop an online banking website application
  • Communicated effectively and gained exposure to agile development methodologies
  • Implemented some front-end and back-end functionality with React and PHP respectively
  • Designed and modeled the relational database with MySQL
